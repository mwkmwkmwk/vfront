Expand description

A helper for reading from a source chunk character by character.

This maintains three pointers into a chunk:

  • The cursor: points at the next character to be read.
  • The end: points at a ‚Äústop point‚Äù past which the cursor cannot advance.
  • The bookmark: points at an arbitrary ‚Äústart point‚Äù at or before the cursor, used for rollback and range construction. Initially points at the same place as the cursor.

A reader can be constructed (by Reader::from) from a SourceRef (sets the cursor to given position, sets the end to the end of the chunk) or a SourceRangeRef (sets the cursor to the start of the range, sets the end to the end of the range).
